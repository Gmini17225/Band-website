About the Festival

The Comox Valley Band & Choir Festival is a non-competitive educational event that brings together middle and secondary school ensembles from across the region.

Over three days, students perform in professional venues, receive feedback from experienced adjudicators, and connect with other young musicians who share their love of music.

Welcome to the Festival

Whether this is your first time attending or you return every year, we're glad you're here. The festival is designed to be a supportive space where students can take musical risks, directors can try new ideas, and families can celebrate student achievement.

Performances are open to families and community members. We encourage you to applaud generously, listen respectfully, and enjoy the variety of ensembles that take the stage.

Festival Sponsors

The Comox Valley Band & Choir Festival is made possible through the generous support of our community partners:

  • Comox Valley School District
  • Local music stores and instrument repair shops
  • Community arts councils and service clubs
  • Parent advisory councils and school music supporters

Thank you to all our sponsors for helping create meaningful musical experiences for students.
